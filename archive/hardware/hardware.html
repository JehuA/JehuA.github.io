Phone

  • Samsung s10+
    Pros
    I really the screen and the camera. The hardware does not seem to slow down like iPhones do with each generation. In the future, if I do not get a new phone, I can see myself installing a new OS on it because I can. It is what you expect out of the phone otherwise. I prefer Android over Apple because I like customizing and tinkering with my hardware and software and that is something I cannot do on Apple without a jailbreak. USB C is also a godsend comapred to lightning chargers or microUSB.
    Cons
    Honestly, not much other than all the proprietary software included inside of the phone. Unless it is unlocked, there is a lot of carrier apps that come with it. In addition, you have have both Samsung and Google shoving both their apps that do the same thing down your throat. In addititon, while I like the screen, I think modern phones' sizes are a little too big. I have no reason to have such a big screen. In my opinion, the iPhone 6s was the perfect size for me. In the future I wil probably not get a "plus" or "pro" version of a phone.
