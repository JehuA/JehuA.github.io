Colorscheme

The colorscheme that I personal use in all my work environments, including terminals, IDEs, text editors, and wherever I can is called Dracula . It keeps everything very color coordinated and unlike other colorschemes, such as Monokai and Gruvbox, is that it is bother pleasing to the eye and also readable. I find that my eyes love the contrasting yet gentle colors. The colorscheme and its hex codes are available online and it is what I use inside my code for the website.

If this color scheme is not your cup of tea, then I do recommend Nord or One Dark. Nord is very easy on the eyes and One Dark has amazing contrast!
